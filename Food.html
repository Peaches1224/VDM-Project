Veggtable Stew

What you need

  • Crock Pot
  • Rice Cooker

ingredients

  • Bundle of carrots
  • Bundle of celery
  • 1 onion
  • 4 potatoes
  • 1 bag of rice
  • 3 cans of tomato soup
  • Your choice of seasoning

Directions

Wash your rice until rice water is clear. 1 cup of rice equals two cups of water. Place in ricer cooker.

Add a cup of water along with the tomato soup in crock pot, season as you like and stir. Set crock pot temperature (Low is 5-6 hours High is 7-8 hours).

When done add your rice to a bowel and place your stew on top. You can also replace the rice for a pot roast.
